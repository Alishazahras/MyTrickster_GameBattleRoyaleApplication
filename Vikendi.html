Vikendi

Description

In the island's heart sits the Mount Kreznic, an imposing mountain towering above the surrounding white-blanketed hills and forests. To the north sprawls the abandoned Cosmodrome complex, its massive domed structures and hangars housing an array of high-tech equipment and military hardware for the taking. Along Vikendi's frozen coastal shores, small towns bustle with activity amidst a flurry of gunfire and chaos. Bitter winds whip through the abandoned ski resorts nestled high up on secluded peaks, their empty hotels and lodges betraying no signs of the intense battles unfolding within.
